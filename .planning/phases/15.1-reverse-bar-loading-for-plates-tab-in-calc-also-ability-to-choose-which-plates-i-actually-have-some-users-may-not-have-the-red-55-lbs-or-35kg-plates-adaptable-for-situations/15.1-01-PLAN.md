       - `calculateTotalWeight(plateCounts: Record<number, number>, barWeight: number): number` — sum per-side weights * 2 + bar. Use `Math.round(total * 100) / 100` to avoid floating point issues.
       - `countsToBreakdown(plateCounts: Record<number, number>): { weight: number; count: number }[]` — filter zeros, sort descending by weight, return array matching PlateBreakdown.plates format.

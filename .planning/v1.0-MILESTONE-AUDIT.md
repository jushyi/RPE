## Cross-Phase Integration Results

**Connected:** 47/52 cross-phase exports properly wired
**Broken flows:** 4 out of 13 E2E flows have confirmed breaks

### Integration Gaps

1. **clearAllUserData() scope** -- Missing 4 MMKV stores (social-store, chat-store, friendship-store, notification-storage). Previous user data leaks on sign-out.

2. **deepLinkRouter missing types** -- `group_share` (Phase 17) and `chat_message` (Phase 18) notification types not handled. Tapping these notifications produces no navigation.

3. **Video upload queue retry** -- `flushVideoQueue` only called in `finishWorkout()`. No auto-retry on app restart or connectivity restore (unlike `useSyncQueue` for workout data).

4. **Body metrics unit defaults** -- `body-metrics.tsx` hardcodes `'in'`/`'lbs'` instead of reading `authStore.preferredUnit`/`preferredMeasurementUnit`.

5. **Alarm notification deep link** -- Tapping alarm/nudge notification routes to `/(app)/workout` but the workout screen bounces back immediately if no active session exists and no `plan_day_id` is in the notification payload.
